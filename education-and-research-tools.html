❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are these tools really free? +

Yes, all tools listed have free access. Some may offer additional paid features, but the core services are free.

Are the courses on Coursera certified? +

Yes, many courses offer certificates upon completion. Some certificates may require a payment, but the course material can be accessed for free.

Is Sci-Hub legal to use? +

Sci-Hub provides access to paywalled content. Its legality varies by country, so use it with awareness of local regulations.

Can I use these tools on mobile? +

Absolutely! Most platforms are mobile-friendly and have apps as well.
